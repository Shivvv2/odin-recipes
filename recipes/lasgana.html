lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made by layering wide sheets of pasta
with a rich meat or vegetable sauce,
creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and plenty of melted mozzarella and Parmesan.
The layers are baked until golden and bubbling, creating a hearty meal with a
perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Known for its comforting and satisfying taste, lasagna combines savory
tomato sauce, tender pasta, and gooey cheese in every bite.
It is a popular choice for family dinners and special occasions,
and it can be customized with ingredients like spinach, mushrooms,
chicken, or beef to suit different preferences.

Ingredients:

  • Thin layer of meat sauce
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Béchamel (or ricotta mixture)
  • Mozzarella
  • Meat sauce
  • Parmesan
  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Make the béchamel sauce
  3. Prepare the pasta
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake
